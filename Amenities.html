Amenities We offer

Welcome to Atwood gym, where we provide everything you need for an exceptional fitness experience. Discover our wide range of amenities designed to help you achieve your fitness goals and enjoy your time at the gym.

Some Amenities that we offer:

  • Secure Lockers: Keep your belongings safe with our secure locker facilities.
  • Shower Facilities: Freshen up after your workout in our clean and well-maintained showers, complete with complimentary toiletries and towel service.
  • Sauna and Steam Room: Relax and detoxify in our luxurious sauna and steam room.
  • Hot Tub: Unwind and soothe your muscles in our spacious hot tub.
  • Kids' Play Area: Keep your little ones entertained and safe in our supervised childcare area while you work out
  • Juice Bar: Refuel with a delicious and nutritious smoothie or snack from our on-site juice bar.
  • Nutrition Counseling: Meet with our nutrition experts to create a balanced diet plan that complements your fitness routine.
