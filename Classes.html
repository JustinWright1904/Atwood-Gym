Classes

Yoga

classes focus on improving flexibility, strength, and mental well-being through a series of poses, breathing exercises, and meditation techniques. Classes may vary from gentle and restorative to more dynamic and challenging styles like Vinyasa or Power Yoga.

Pilates:

classes emphasize core strength, stability, and overall body conditioning through controlled movements and precise alignment. Participants often use specialized equipment like reformers or perform mat-based exercises.

Zumba:

Zumba is a high-energy dance fitness class inspired by Latin rhythms and international music. Participants follow choreographed dance routines that incorporate aerobic and interval training for a fun and effective workout.

Spinning/Cycling:

Spinning or cycling classes are indoor cycling workouts performed on stationary bikes. Led by an instructor, participants pedal to the rhythm of music while varying resistance levels to simulate different terrains and intensities.

(High-Intensity Interval Training):

classes involve short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief rest periods, designed to maximize calorie burn and improve cardiovascular fitness. These fast-paced workouts typically combine cardio and strength exercises for a full-body challenge.
